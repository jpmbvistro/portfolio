About Me

Born in the Philippines, I moved to the US at a young age. With an early interest in technology and engineering, I worked several software internships before my first year of college. Utilizing my early foundation in computer science, I established a grounding in data abstraction and discrete thinking.

While investing time into my local community, I established and lead a Filipinx organization, and served as a Programs Director and Program Lead for the longest-running Asian American open mic at a local nonprofit. My grassroots events averaged over 40-50 attendees and garnered the support of local marginalized communities. During my time with these organizations, I honed a reflective analysis and cultural competence in my programmatic design.

I utilize these experiences to craft complex yet elegant software solutions.
